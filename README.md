# A2 Cow Health & Wellness Guide

Welcome to the official repository for **A2 Cow Health**. This project is dedicated to sharing knowledge and best practices for maintaining the health of indigenous A2 cows, ensuring the highest quality of natural dairy products.

## 🌿 Our Mission
To promote traditional and ethical Vedic farming practices that prioritize cow comfort and natural well-being.

## 📋 Key Focus Areas
* **Nutritional Care:** Balanced diet plans including green fodder and natural supplements.
* **Preventive Health:** Traditional Ayurvedic treatments for cattle.
* **A2 Product Standards:** Guidelines for producing pure A2 Bilona Ghee and raw honey.
